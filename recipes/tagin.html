Tagin

The morrocan Tagin

A tajine or tagine (Arabic: طاجين) is a North African dish, named after the earthenware pot in which it is cooked. It is also called maraq or marqa.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Moroccan spices

Steps

  1. Arrange the Base Layer
  2. Add the Garlic
  3. Add Olive Oil
  4. Arrange the Chicken in the Center
  5. Mix the Moroccan Spices
  6. Season the Chicken and Onions
  7. Arrange the Vegetables and Season
  8. Garnish the Tagin
  9. Add Water to the Tagin
  10. Cook the Tagin
